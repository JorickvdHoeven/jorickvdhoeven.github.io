Did you know that you could search for hidden characters and replace with hidden characters in excel?

Simply type:
`alt + [ASCII Code]`
To find any non-printable characters.


Here are some handy Ascii codes:
`0010: Carriage return (\r)`
`0013: Line Feed (\n)`
`0011: Tab (\t)`
